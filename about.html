About Us

About Us

Chris Visser, the company's director, established Jcs Auto Mech in partnership with Bosch Service. The company is located at 32 Farmwell Building on Vos Street, the main road in Ceres.


Our goal is to meet the needs of our customers by providing quality service not normally found in today's market place. Jcs Auto Mech has been built to give the customer a sense of security and assurance knowing that they are dealing with legitimate people.


When you come to Jcs Auto Mech expect the best. We are your one-stop shop for for all your vehicle maintenance needs. From basic oil changes to advanced mechanical services, we have you covered. Trust us for all your auto repair and servicing.


Jcs Auto Mech is the place where your vehicle gets the specialized repair it deserves. A place where you are treated with respect and honesty. A place you can be assured that when we tell you it's ready, it actually is, because we don't rush through the job.


At Jcs Auto Mech, we're all family -and if you're part of our family, you're in great hands.
